# knowledge/competitive

Competitive intelligence: objections heard in the field, counter-positioning, real pricing comparisons.

## What belongs here

- Objection handling guides by competitor
- Real customer quotes about competitor pain points
- Verified pricing comparisons (with dates)
- Feature gap tracking (where DO is behind and workarounds)

## File format

```markdown
# Competitive: [Competitor Name]

updated: YYYY-MM-DD

## Their strengths (be honest)
## Their weaknesses
## Common objections and responses
## Where we win
## Where we lose (and what to do)
## Pricing comparison
## Field notes from engagements
```

## Ground rules

- Be factual. Don't fabricate competitor weaknesses.
- Always date pricing data — it goes stale fast.
- Note the source (customer quote, public pricing page, internal research).
